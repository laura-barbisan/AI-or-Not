Is this fun-fact true?

Regal Horned Lizard

"The regal horned lizard squirts blood out of its eyesockets to frighten and repel predators."

This fun-fact is true! The blood can travel a distance of up to 3 feet and is noxious to dogs, wolves and coyotes.

Giant Panda

"Giant panda males will sometimes do handstands to pee so they can leave their scent higher up on a tree."

Elephant

This fun-fact is true! Elephants usually develop this preference at around 3-months-old.
